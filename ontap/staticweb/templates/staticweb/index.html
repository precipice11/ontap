OnTap

Fresh Drinking Water Locator

Welcome to OnTap, the easiest way to find fresh drinking water fountains around you. Whether you're on the go, hiking, biking, or simply out for a walk, OnTap helps you locate the nearest public drinking fountains and water refill stations in your area.

Australia's First Drinking Fountain Locating Application

OnTap is Australia's first mobile application designed to locate public drinking fountains, ensuring that clean and fresh water is always within reach. Say goodbye to plastic bottles and stay hydrated sustainably.

The App

OnTap provides users with a simple, intuitive, and efficient way to locate water fountains and refill stations. With its user-friendly interface, the app includes the following features:

Real-time Fountain Locations

Find drinking fountains near you in real-time, with information about their condition, accessibility, and user ratings.

Map & List Views

View fountains on an interactive map or list, sortable by proximity, condition rating, or name.

Detailed View

Discover more information about the fountain, such as a community based condition rating, upload date, as well as directions.

User Contributions

Contribute by adding new water sources to the app for community benefit.
