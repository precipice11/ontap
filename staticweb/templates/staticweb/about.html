About OnTap

OnTap is a mobile application designed to help users easily locate fresh and accessible drinking water in their local area. Launched in 2023, OnTap is driven by the goal of reducing plastic waste, promoting sustainable water consumption, and ensuring that clean drinking water is always within reach for everyone.

Our Story

Founded by Rayan Master, OnTap was developed to solve a growing problem in urban areas â€” the difficulty of locating safe and convenient water sources. Recognizing the environmental impact of single-use plastic bottles and the need for accessible water, OnTap was created to connect people to nearby drinking fountains and refill stations through an intuitive and user-friendly platform.

Our Mission

At OnTap, our mission is simple: to make clean drinking water accessible to everyone, while promoting sustainable practices and reducing reliance on single-use plastics. By providing a reliable solution for finding water fountains and refill stations, OnTap empowers individuals to stay hydrated sustainably, helping both people and the planet.

Founder and Developer

Rayan Master, the visionary behind OnTap, is passionate about creating solutions that address real-world challenges. Combining innovation, sustainability, and usability, Rayan developed OnTap to improve access to clean drinking water and inspire environmentally responsible habits.
